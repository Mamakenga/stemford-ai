# PLAN Stemford: Three Echelons

Version: 1.0  
Date: 2026-03-17  
Status: Active

## 1) Purpose

This is a separate practical plan for Stemford development in three echelons:
1. Repositioning
2. Rebranding
3. Operational modernization

The priority is real business progress inside Stemford.  
Scaling to other clients is not a current priority.

## 2) Core Logic

The order is strict:
1. First define who we are and why parents should choose us (Echelon 1).
2. Then change brand shell to match this meaning (Echelon 2).
3. Then optimize operations under the new identity (Echelon 3).

If this order is broken, we risk beautiful branding with weak strategy.

## 3) Echelon 1: Repositioning (First Priority)

Goal: define Stemford identity without franchise dependency and create strategic foundation.

### 3.1 Workstreams

1. Independent identity of the school (mission, role, promise).
2. Unique value proposition (what we give that competitors do not).
3. Target audience and parent value proposition.
4. Student retention strategy during brand transition.
5. Parent communication strategy ("why we are changing and what this means for you").
6. Competitive landscape analysis in Bulgaria (kids IT education segment).
7. Curriculum audit:
   - franchise-dependent parts,
   - own assets,
   - development priorities.
8. Legal de-coupling from franchise with risk minimization.

### 3.2 Mandatory Outputs (Definition of Done)

1. `A1 Identity & Mission Brief`
2. `A2 UVP & Positioning Statement`
3. `A3 Target Segments & Parent Value Map`
4. `A4 Retention Plan During Transition`
5. `A5 Parent Communication Pack (FAQ + scripts + timeline)`
6. `A6 Competitive Landscape Report (Bulgaria)`
7. `A7 Curriculum Ownership Matrix`
8. `A8 Legal De-coupling Checklist + risk map`

### 3.3 Exit Gate (before Echelon 2)

Move to Echelon 2 only if:
1. All A1–A8 artifacts are complete and approved by CEO.
2. Parent communication package is ready for real rollout.
3. Legal risk level is documented and acceptable.
4. Retention risk mitigation actions are scheduled.

## 4) Echelon 2: Rebranding

Goal: reflect approved repositioning in all visible brand assets.

### 4.1 Scope

1. New naming architecture.
2. Visual identity and design system.
3. Website, social channels, printed materials, signage.
4. Contract/document templates update.
5. Replacement of all legacy KIBERone-bound elements.

### 4.2 Mandatory Outputs

1. `B1 Brand Platform (name, tone, promise)`
2. `B2 Visual System (logo/colors/type/layout rules)`
3. `B3 Website + Social Rebrand Kit`
4. `B4 Offline Assets Pack (print/signage/classroom)`
5. `B5 Legal/Document Brand Migration Pack`

### 4.3 Exit Gate (before Echelon 3)

1. 100% core parent-facing assets migrated.
2. Contract/doc templates migrated and validated.
3. Internal team can execute brand rules without ambiguity.

## 5) Echelon 3: Operational Modernization

Goal: strengthen execution quality and economics under the new brand.

### 5.1 Scope

1. Process standardization.
2. Automation and control-plane maturity.
3. Financial analytics and KPI discipline.
4. Product and program development loop.

### 5.2 Mandatory Outputs

1. `C1 Process SOP Pack`
2. `C2 Automation Backlog + Implemented Flows`
3. `C3 Financial Dashboard (unit economics + cohort/retention)`
4. `C4 Product Development Roadmap`

## 6) AI Deputy Department Adaptation

### 6.1 Roles for Echelon 1 (active now)

1. `strategy-gatekeeper`: identity, UVP, competitive position, strategic consistency.
2. `pmo`: timeline orchestration, parent communication rollout, retention execution.
3. `finance-kpi`: value economics, retention/cost impact, pricing implications.
4. `orchestrator`: stage control, task routing, decision gates, risk escalation.

### 6.2 Working Cadence

1. Weekly planning: priorities for current echelon only.
2. Daily execution: tasks + blockers + approvals.
3. Weekly CEO summary:
   - what was done,
   - what is blocked,
   - what decision is needed from CEO.

## 7) Priority Rule

Until Echelon 1 exit gate is passed:
1. all high-priority tasks must support A1–A8;
2. non-critical side initiatives go to backlog;
3. technical changes are accepted only if they accelerate Echelon 1 execution.

## 8) Immediate Next Step

Create and launch Stage A backlog in Control API with 8 tasks (A1–A8), each with:
1. owner role,
2. acceptance criteria,
3. target deadline,
4. dependency links.
