# PLAN: Service Productization

Status: Active Canon  
Version: 1.0  
Date: 2026-03-28  
Scope: third product layer, reusable service for other businesses

## 1) Purpose

Generalize the system into a reusable service after:
1. coder factory is stable
2. Stemford business control plane is proven in real work

## 2) Product promise

Offer a configurable AI operations system for different businesses, where:
1. roles can be adapted
2. dashboards can be adapted
3. workflows can be adapted
4. approvals and safety remain formal

## 3) Core packaging units

1. engine
2. role templates
3. dashboard templates
4. approval policies
5. deployment runbooks
6. onboarding checklist for new client

## 4) Dependency

This plan is explicitly blocked until:
1. coder factory proves the engineering loop
2. Stemford proves the business loop

## 5) Definition of done

Service productization is real only if:
1. a new business can be onboarded by configuration, not by reinvention
2. the split between coder dashboard and business dashboard remains intact
3. client-specific logic does not break the shared engine
